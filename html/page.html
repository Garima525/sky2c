Welcome to Sky2C Freight Systems Inc!

Sky2C is a US based company dedicated to providing businesses across diverse industry verticals and individuals worldwide. Sky2C offers a highly integrated, world class and affordable 360º shipping solution in the fastest possible time frame.

Comprehensive Suite of Services

At Sky2C, we offer our clients a comprehensive suite of shipping solutions and services including
  • International shipping
  • Freight forwarding
  • Domestic shipping
  • Warehousing
  • Consolidation services
  • FTL and LTL Trucking services
  • Heavy lift transportation services
  • Door-2-Door pickup and delivery services
  • Special project handling
  • Trade Show services and many more

We are your one-stop shop for all your shipping needs both in terms of facilities, manpower, and resources and off course, best competitive market rates.

Licensed Company

Sky2C is a licensed company, holding all necessary government licenses for both custom clearance and operations of a fleet of vehicles for collection and deliveries.

Years of Experience and a Rapidly Growing Network

We take great pride and honor in being the first choice of preference amongst worldwide customers. Sky2C pillars on a dedicated team of highly skilled, experienced and well qualified professionals. Hence exhibiting a perfect combination to our undisputed professionalism and years of hands-on experience in the shipping industry. A great line-up of all necessary infrastructure and resources enables us to handle and take care of all your logistical needs with reliability, speed and accuracy. Ensuring shipments reach their final destination on timely and without any delays, is primus to Sky2C.

World-class services and a rapidly growing customer base, has earned Sky2C the reputation of being the epitome in the shipping industry with a strong competitive edge.

Compliance With Industry Rules and Regulations

To ensure we maintain and sustain the delivery of highest quality of services every time and always, Sky2C strictly complies with all governing rules and regulations in the moving and freight forwarding industry.

Strong Association with Established Agents Worldwide

A strong association with established agents worldwide enables Sky2C to provide a wide range of shipping solutions and services to all customers globally. We are able to plan, arrange, co-ordinate and control the shipment of materials, project cargo and equipment from one location to the other, in a timely manner.

Strong Customer Base

A phenomenal increase in customers, year after year, has significantly attributed towards the success of Sky2C and the growth in customer satisfaction. After successfully providing quality and professional shipping, transportation and logistics solutions to clients in the US and India, Sky2C now has a customer base which is rapidly growing across the globe and in countries including Philippines, Africa, Saudi Arabia, Ghana, Singapore, Kenya, Dubai, Tanzania, Pakistan, Malaysia, Australia, South Korea and Indonesia.

Our Mission

Providing our customers, comprehensive, affordable and quality shipping solutions by incorporating new shipping concepts to meet industry challenges and dynamic market situations smoothly and effectively.
